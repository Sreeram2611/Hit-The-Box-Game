About the Game

Hit The Box is a fun turn-based reflex game where the player and computer take turns calling out months ‚Äî from January to December. The player who says December must try to "hit" the opponent. The opponent then gets a chance to escape by selecting the correct action (Jump, Duck, etc.).

If the hit succeeds, the attacker wins. If the escape is correct, the defender wins!

This game was designed for fun, timing, and quick decision-making. Enjoy!
